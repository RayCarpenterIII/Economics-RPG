# Changelog

## v34 - HD-2D Massive Migration

- Added 32-pixel art contract over the preserved 16-unit simulation grid.
- Added 640x360 logical viewport and 1280x720 presentation output.
- Added foot-anchored larger characters, compact colliders, depth sorting, shadows, foreground layers, lighting, atmosphere, and asset metadata.
- Added visual registry, runtime custom asset support, asset validation, debug tools, and standalone-export support.
- Advanced save compatibility to version 34.

## v33 - Workshop Production Chains

- Added sawmill, mason yard, bloomery, mill, bakehouse, and artisan workshop.
- Added timed intermediate production, worker assignment, agricultural conversion, bottlenecks, and price effects.

## v32 - NPC Conversation Intelligence

- Added fact-first deterministic dialogue, stable voices, topic memory, response caching, and constrained optional local-LLM phrasing.

## v31 - Social Dynamics

- Added friendships, loyalty, grudges, rivalries, romance, courtship, political factions, and family reactions.

## v30 - NPC Continuity

- Added persistent life goals, risk tolerance, opinions, emotional memories, moods, and event reactions.

## v29 - Terrain Infrastructure

- Added player-built paths and bridges, engineering, and villager-built public roads.

## v28 - Biomes and Elevation

- Added deterministic ecology, terrain-aware movement and pathfinding, gathering, agriculture, and wildlife habitat.

## v27 - Pen and Animal Visual Fixes

## v26 - Living Agriculture

## v25 - Pathfinding and Music

## v24 - Living Construction

## v23 - Hidden Fish Ecology
