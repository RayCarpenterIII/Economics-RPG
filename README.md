# The Egg Lands v0.27 — Trade Ally Escort

v0.27 converts the existing trade-ally protection pledge into active gameplay while preserving the v0.26 Python-authoritative living economy.

## Full local run

Launch `start_game.bat` on Windows or `./start_game.sh` on macOS/Linux.

Python continues to own:

- Town prices and inventories
- Production and real input chains
- Employment and unemployment
- Wages and payroll
- Household consumption and unmet demand
- Development-gated formal markets
- Direct villager demand and offers
- Trade relationships and protection pledges
- The three-territory internal empire market

## New in v0.27

A villager who has crossed the existing protection threshold can now be invited into the player's escort from the villager dialogue panel.

Active trade allies:

- Follow the character in the world and dungeons
- Recall automatically if separated by a large distance
- Detect and engage nearby enemies
- Deal and receive combat damage
- Become temporarily downed instead of being permanently deleted
- Recover and resume escort duty
- Persist active state, health, and recovery state through saves
- Can be recalled or dismissed through mouse/touch controls in Settings

The first release supports up to two simultaneous escorts to limit crowding and update cost.

## Standalone HTML

The same HTML remains playable without Python. The standalone demand economy, direct villager trade, relationships, escort behavior, character creation, combat, mobile controls, raptors, saves, and byte-backed raptor audio remain available.

## Current boundary

Trade allies now provide nearby autonomous escort and combat support. Strategic formations, ally equipment, squad roles, and large-party command are not yet implemented. Conquered-town control and internal market pooling exist, while the complete raid-to-annexation campaign loop remains future government/combat work.
