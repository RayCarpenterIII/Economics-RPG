How to play

WASD / arrowsWalk through the valley and dungeon. ETalk, trade, build, enter the mine, or use equipped gathering tools. ResourcesBuy a rod, axe, pickaxe, or salvage kit at a market. Fish water, chop trees, mine mountain faces, and recover monster parts only from defeated monsters. BackpackEvery stack and item has a grid footprint. Click crafted gear in the bag to equip it; click an equipped slot to pack it again. CraftingGeneral recipes work anywhere. Enter a Watchpost, Academy Annex, or Civic Court to craft its unique Specialty equipment. MouseAim freely. Left-click attacks toward the reticle. SpaceAttack toward your aim; time presses for a three-hit combo. F / QUse your combat Specialty or daily economic influence. ShiftDash. C / right-clickBlock toward your aim. A last-moment block parries and bounces the attacker back. M / MenuOpen Inventory first, then switch among Map, Skills, Crafting, Chronicle, Help, and Home Town. KOpen the Menu directly to Skills. Phone landscapeThe translucent HUD appears automatically. Left pad moves; Attack auto-aims; hold Block to defend; Use gathers or interacts. ⛶On a phone, request browser fullscreen while keeping the landscape HUD. GoalTrade among three towns, keep a caravan alive, recover the Heartstone, and construct a civic building.
