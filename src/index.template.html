How to play

WASD / arrowsWalk through the valley and dungeon. ETalk, trade, build, enter the mine, or use equipped gathering tools. ResourcesBuy a rod, axe, pickaxe, or salvage kit at a market. Trees yield logs and disappear when felled; rocks yield stone and break into rubble. Both regrow after several days. BackpackEvery stack and item has a grid footprint. Click crafted gear in the bag to equip it; click an equipped slot to pack it again. CraftingGeneral recipes work anywhere. Enter a Watchpost, Academy Annex, or Civic Court to craft its unique Specialty equipment. MouseAim freely. Left-click attacks toward the reticle. SpaceAttack toward your aim; time presses for a three-hit combo. F / QUse your combat Specialty or daily economic influence. XJump. Press X again in the air to double-jump over low attacks. ShiftDash. Dashing during a jump keeps your aerial momentum. C / right-clickBlock toward your aim with a shield equipped. A last-moment block parries and bounces the attacker back. M / MenuOpen your character page first, then switch among Bag, Map, Skills, Crafting, Relations, Journal, Town, and more. GovernmentChosen at character creation and managed inside the Town tab: rule type, town focus, relations, and raids. Fog of warUnexplored land is dark in the world and on the map. It clears permanently as you explore, and saves remember it. KOpen the Menu directly to Skills. Phone landscapeLeft pad moves; double-tap a direction to dash. Attack strikes, Block guards and can parry, Skill uses your Specialty (hold for influence), and Jump can be pressed twice. ⛶On a phone, request browser fullscreen while keeping the landscape HUD. GoalTrade among three towns, keep a caravan alive, recover the Heartstone, and construct a civic building.
